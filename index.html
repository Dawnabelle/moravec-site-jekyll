We consider the story that the software needs to tell as well as the story the map is telling.

Software exists all around us. It has become part of our day to day lives. We have apps on our computers, on our phones, on our watches. Even on our glasses. Some software is good and some is not. How do we differentiate between good and not so good software? At Moravec Labs, we believe good software tells a story. It is intuitive. Users don’t need to understand all the aspects of an application to use it.

Maps are similar. They convey a story. The better the map, the more informative the story. Good maps, like good software, should convey meaning and should be easily understood.

That is what makes Software Development with a touch of Maps so interesting. We consider the story that the software needs to tell as well as the story the map is telling. Are the stories the same? Do they complement each other? At Moravec Labs, we weave these stories together to tell a comprehensive and fluid story for the user.
